Common Refacing Questions

What Broadview Heights Homeowners Ask First

What is the difference between refacing and replacing?
Replacing requires tearing out every single structural box, destroying your drywall, and moving plumbing lines. Refacing keeps your existing, sturdy cabinet boxes perfectly intact. We simply strip and replace the exterior surfaces, doors, and hardware—giving you a brand-new custom look in days for a fraction of the cost.
Can I upgrade my countertops and backsplashes at the same time?
Absolutely. Most Broadview Heights homeowners choose our "Builder-Grade Overhaul" package, where we pair custom cabinet refacing with premium quartz countertops and beautiful new tile backsplashes all in one seamless visit.
How long will my kitchen be out of service?
Hardly at all. Traditional gut-remodels leave you without a working sink or stove for weeks. Because refacing works directly on your existing footprint, your kitchen remains fully functional throughout the brief 3-to-5-day process.
