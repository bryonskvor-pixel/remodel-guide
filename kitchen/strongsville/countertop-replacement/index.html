The Surface Blueprint

The Triple Upgrade Package: Countertop, Backsplash, & Sink

True visual transformation happens when the entire workspace is upgraded simultaneously. Here is why combining these three elements yields a flawless custom result.

01 · Premium Quartz Installation

We replace your stained builder granite or peeling laminate with non-porous, maintenance-free luxury quartz. It resists heat, scratches, and bacteria perfectly, giving your island and counters an expensive, continuous slab aesthetic.

02 · Custom Tile Backsplash

Ditch the plain drywall or short, continuous 4-inch stone lips. We run beautiful custom ceramic, porcelain, or glass tiles from your new quartz line straight up to the bottom of your upper cabinetry, creating an immediate visual anchor.

03 · Under-Mount Sink & Pro Faucet

Tearing out counters allows us to eliminate old drop-in rims. We install deep, seamless under-mount stainless or composite basins paired with professional commercial gooseneck pull-down faucets for an absolute leap in daily kitchen mechanics.

Common Surface Questions

What Strongsville Homeowners Ask First

Can you replace my countertops without changing my cabinets?
Yes, this is our specialty. As long as your existing base cabinet boxes are structurally solid and level, we remove old tops and install massive, heavy quartz slabs safely without causing any harm to your existing frames.
How long does the countertop and tile backsplash process take?
Once your custom quartz slabs are fully fabricated, the on-site physical installation of the counters, under-mount sink plumbing, and custom tile installation takes roughly 2 to 3 days to complete. You face almost zero lifestyle down-time.
Do I need to buy the under-mount sink and faucet fixtures myself?
You can provide your own if you have a highly specific look in mind, but we supply premium, commercial-grade under-mount stainless steel or dense granite composite basins and heavy single-handle gooseneck pull-down faucets as part of our high-value packages. We take care of all measurements to guarantee a perfect drop-in fit.
