Makeover Questions

What Strongsville Homeowners Ask First

Why is replacing the doors better than just painting my old ones?
Painting alone cannot hide outdated profiles or cathedral arches. Old doors are often warped, heavily grained, or saturated with kitchen grease after decades of use. Swapping out the doors gives you clean, flat surfaces and modern shaker styles, while painting only the boxes ensures a pristine, factory-fresh finish across the entire kitchen.
Can we add premium counters or an under-mount sink to this package?
Absolutely. Many Strongsville families pair this hybrid door and painting upgrade with solid quartz countertops, a new tile backsplash, and a modern under-mount sink/faucet system for a complete Level 4 overhaul.
How long will my kitchen be out of service?
Hardly at all. Traditional gut-remodels leave you without a kitchen for a month or more. Because our hybrid system works directly within your existing layout framework, your kitchen remains fully active throughout the brief 3-to-4-day process.
