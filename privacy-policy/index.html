Privacy Policy

remodel.guide  ·  Effective Date: June 25, 2026

About This Site

remodel.guide publishes free homeowner remodeling guides — kitchen, flooring, bathroom, basement, roofing, and more — for residents of Northeast Ohio. The site is operated by Remodelry (Clarity Companion LLC), a residential remodeling concierge service based in Lorain, Cuyahoga, and Medina Counties.

This site also offers a free consultation request form to connect homeowners with Bryon Skvor at Remodelry.

Information We Collect

When you submit a consultation request form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • City or neighborhood
  • Project type and scope
  • Desired timeline
  • SMS opt-in preference

We do not collect payment information. No account creation is required.

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your remodeling project inquiry.
  • To send an email confirmation of your request.
  • To send SMS messages if you opted in.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third-party marketers.

Cookies and Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4), which sets cookies to measure anonymous site traffic — pages visited, time on site, and browser type. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or cookies that track you across other websites.

Third-Party Services

  • Vercel — Hosting. May log IP addresses and request metadata. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Airtable — Form submissions are stored in a private Airtable base accessible only to Remodelry. See Airtable's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Email confirmation delivery.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous site traffic measurement. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Consultation request data is retained in Airtable until the inquiry is resolved or you request deletion. To request deletion of your submitted information, contact us at bryon@claritycompanionllc.com.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of any information you submitted through a form on this site. Contact us at bryon@claritycompanionllc.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
