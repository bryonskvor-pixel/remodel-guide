Accepted Items

What We Will & Won't Take

We handle the vast majority of residential and commercial debris. A few categories require special handling — here's the honest list.

✅

We Take All of This

  • Furniture & mattresses
  • Appliances (washer, dryer, stove)
  • Electronics & TVs
  • Yard waste & debris
  • Construction materials
  • Carpeting & flooring
  • Hot tubs & sheds
  • Exercise equipment
  • Clothing & household goods
  • Office furniture
  • Bicycles & sporting goods
  • Boxes & general junk
  • Drywall & lumber
  • Concrete & brick (call first)
Items in good condition are donated or recycled when possible.
🚫

We Cannot Accept

  • Hazardous chemicals
  • Paint (unless dried solid)
  • Motor oil & fuel
  • Pesticides & herbicides
  • Asbestos materials
  • Medical / biohazard waste
  • Propane tanks (full)
  • Vehicle batteries
  • Refrigerators with Freon*
  • Fluorescent light bulbs
  • Tires (call — may apply fee)
  • Railroad ties
*Refrigerators & AC units can be accepted after Freon is certified-removed. Call us for guidance on disposal options for restricted items.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need to be home during the pickup?

Not necessarily. If items are accessible and payment is arranged, we can complete many jobs without you on-site. We'll send a photo confirmation when done.

How quickly can you come out?

Same-day availability is common, especially on weekdays. We'll always give you a confirmed window — usually a 2-hour arrival range.

Do you donate or recycle anything?

Yes. Furniture, clothing, and usable household goods are donated when possible. We divert as much from the landfill as we can.

How does dumpster rental work?

You book a container, we deliver it to your driveway or job site, you fill it on your schedule, and we haul it away when you're ready. Simple.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Legacy Junk Removal LLC carries full liability insurance. Certificates of insurance are available for contractors upon request.

How is the price determined?

Volume and weight of the load, labor required, and haul distance. We give you a firm quote before we start — no surprises at the end.

Can I book a recurring or ongoing contract?

Absolutely. Property managers, real estate investors, and contractors with ongoing work can set up accounts with scheduled service and contractor rates.

What about heavy items like concrete or dirt?

We can handle them — but heavy debris is priced separately due to disposal costs. Call us first so we can quote accurately and bring the right equipment.
