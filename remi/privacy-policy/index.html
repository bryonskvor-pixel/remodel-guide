Privacy Policy

remodelry.guide  ·  Effective Date: June 25, 2026

About This Site

remodelry.guide is an AI-powered homeowner intake tool operated by Remodelry, a residential remodeling concierge service based in Northeast Ohio (Lorain, Cuyahoga, and Medina Counties).

The core feature is Remi, an AI that guides homeowners through a structured project intake conversation, collects project details, analyzes behavioral and communication preferences (SoulMap scoring), and generates two output documents: a contractor-ready project brief and a homeowner summary. The site also includes 120+ geo-targeted landing pages serving the Cleveland metropolitan area.

Information We Collect

When you use the Remi intake tool, we collect the information you voluntarily provide during the conversation, including:

  • Project type and scope (e.g., kitchen remodel, basement finishing)
  • Project timeline and desired start date
  • Budget range
  • Location (city or neighborhood)
  • Behavioral and communication style preferences (used for SoulMap scoring)
  • Contact information you choose to share (name, phone, email)

This information is stored in Airtable and used to generate your project brief and homeowner summary documents. No payment information is collected. You do not need to create an account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your contractor-ready project brief and homeowner summary via the Remi AI.
  • To deliver your project documents and follow up with you about your remodeling project (if you provided contact information).
  • To use project data in aggregate, anonymized form to improve Remi's intake process and SoulMap scoring model — never in a form that identifies you.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Vercel — Hosting and deployment. May log IP addresses and request metadata. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Airtable — Project data storage. Your submitted intake information is stored in a private Airtable base accessible only to Remodelry staff. See Airtable's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — The Remi conversation and document generation is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI models. Your intake responses are transmitted to Anthropic's API for processing. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Email delivery for project documents and notifications.

Cookies

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4), which sets cookies to measure anonymous site traffic. These cookies do not identify you personally and are used solely for aggregate usage statistics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or cookies that track you across other websites.

Data Retention

Project intake data is retained for 90 days after submission, then permanently deleted unless you have requested an active follow-up engagement with Remodelry. If you would like your data deleted sooner, contact us at the address below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the project data we have stored about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data prior to the 90-day automatic deletion window.

To exercise these rights, contact us at bryon@claritycompanionllc.com.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
