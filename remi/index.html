What Remi Is

Not a chatbot. Not a form.
Something genuinely different.

Every homeowner who has filled out a "free estimate" form online knows what happens next. The phone rings — multiple times — from contractors who know nothing about the project and are calling to close a job.

Remi is the opposite of that. Remi is a purpose-built AI intake specialist — trained on construction field knowledge, behavioral psychology, and the Remodelry methodology. She asks about your project the way a skilled professional would: one question at a time, in the right order, listening to what you actually say and responding to it.

She can see photos you upload. She identifies design direction from inspiration images. She asks about the household — not just the project. She captures how you make decisions, what you're worried about, and what success looks like to you. And when the conversation ends, two things happen simultaneously that have never happened before in residential remodeling.

The Behavioral Intelligence Layer

SoulMap — running silently
throughout every conversation.

While Remi talks about your project, she is also reading something else — how you communicate, how you make decisions, what your emotional driver is, and what your relationship with the budget looks like. This behavioral layer runs silently throughout the conversation and produces a named archetype that shapes everything about the Concierge visit.

Dreamer
Vision-driven, wants warmth and validation, needs help landing the direction
Newcomer
First major project, wants reassurance, benefits from binary choices and clear guidance
Investor
Research-driven, confident, respects expertise — lead with competence, not process
Protector
Risk-aware, needs flags named early, values honesty over enthusiasm
Architect
Systems-thinker, wants precision and efficiency, impatient with vagueness

The archetype is never announced to the homeowner. It shapes how Remi asks questions, how the Concierge approaches the visit, and how the Clarity Package is written. A Dreamer gets warmth and vision. An Investor gets competence and specifics. A Protector gets honest flags named early and clearly.

This is the difference between a conversation that feels generic and one that feels like someone actually understood you — before they ever set foot in your home.

Remi is free. There is no sales pitch. No one will call you from a contractor's office. What you will receive is a First Look that is more specific and more useful than anything that comes out of a free estimate call — and a Concierge who already knows your project when they reach out.

What Remi Is Not

A few things worth
saying directly.

✓

Remi is not a generic AI chatbot

Remi runs on Claude — one of the most capable AI models available — with a purpose-built system prompt encoding 20+ years of residential construction field knowledge, behavioral psychology, and the Remodelry methodology. She knows about moisture testing, vapor barriers, egress windows, cabinet lead times, countertop sequencing, SoulMap archetypes, and what questions to ask about a 90s kitchen when someone uploads a photo. She is not general purpose. She is built for this.

✓

Remi is not a lead capture form

Remi does not exist to collect your contact information and sell it to contractors. She exists to produce two documents — one for you, one for the Concierge — that make the entire Remodelry process possible. Your information stays with Remodelry. No contractor receives it without your explicit consent through the Remodelry introduction process.

✓

Remi is not a replacement for the Concierge

Remi cannot see your space. She cannot measure. She cannot assess infrastructure. She cannot read the room the way a person can. What she can do is ensure that the person who does those things arrives already knowing your project — so that the 60 to 90 minutes of the Concierge visit is spent going deeper, not starting from scratch.

✓

Remi is not perfect — and she knows it

Remi is an AI. She occasionally misses nuance, asks a question that could be sharper, or produces a First Look that needs refinement. The Concierge visit is designed to correct and deepen everything Remi captured. The First Look is a starting point, not a final document. Its value is that it exists at all — that you receive something specific and useful within seconds of the conversation ending rather than waiting days for a callback from someone who doesn't know your project.
